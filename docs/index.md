# Fortuna DAO Developer Documentation

Welcome to the official developer documentation for Fortuna DAO. This guide provides all the necessary information to understand, integrate with, and contribute to the Fortuna DAO ecosystem.

## 1. Overview

**Purpose:**
Fortuna DAO is a community-driven decentralized organization (DAO) operating on the XRP Ledger. Its primary mission is to provide a transparent and efficient platform for funding innovative projects proposed by the community. By leveraging the low transaction fees and high speed of the XRPL, Fortuna DAO aims to democratize access to funding and empower creators worldwide.

**Key Objectives:**
- **Empower Creators:** Offer a streamlined process for project creators to submit proposals and secure funding directly from the community.
- **Engage Donors:** Provide a transparent and rewarding experience for donors, allowing them to track the impact of their contributions and participate in governance.
- **Foster Community:** Build a vibrant ecosystem where creators, donors, and developers can collaborate and shape the future of the DAO.
- **Promote Transparency:** Ensure all funding and governance activities are recorded on-chain for complete auditability.
